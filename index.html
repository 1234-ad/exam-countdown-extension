Questions Students Ask

Does the countdown sync across devices?

Yes, sign in with the same browser profile and Exam Countdown keeps your timers, playlists, and wallpaper preferences in sync.

Does Exam Countdown work offline?

Yes! Your countdowns, notes, and wallpapers are cached locally so you can stay on track even during no-wifi study sessions.

Is Exam Countdown open source?

Yes, it is! Feel free to make changes to the code and contribute to the project.
